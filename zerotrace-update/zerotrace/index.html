ABOUT THIS PLATFORM

Zero Trace Cyber Legacy is a structured cybersecurity education platform designed for African learners. Built and delivered as Identified and Remediated, this curriculum covers 6 modules and 60 lessons across the full cybersecurity lifecycle.

From passive reconnaissance to active exploitation to defensive forensics — every lesson is designed to be practical, hands-on, and immediately applicable in the real world.
